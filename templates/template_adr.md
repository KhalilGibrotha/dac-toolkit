> **Document type:** `adr` | **Diátaxis:** — (architectural governance artifact)
>
> An Architecture Decision Record (ADR) captures a significant technical or
> architectural decision: what was decided, what alternatives were considered,
> and what consequences follow. ADRs are immutable once accepted — they are a
> historical record, not a living document. Supersede rather than edit.
>
> ADRs record decisions that *have been made*. If you are still exploring options
> and seeking group input, use `template_proposal.md` first. When the group
> reaches consensus, the proposal outcome is captured here.
>
> **Do not use** for decisions still under discussion — use `template_proposal.md`.
> Do not retroactively clean up or rewrite accepted ADRs; create a new ADR
> that supersedes the old one.
>
> **Suggested location:** `decisions/`
> **File naming:** `adr_[domain]_[short-decision-title].md`
> **Status lifecycle:** Proposed → Accepted → Rejected → Retired
