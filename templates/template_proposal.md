> **Template Note** — Delete this block before publishing.
>
> **Document type:** `proposal` | **Diátaxis:** — (governance artifact)
>
> An Architecture Proposal is a lightweight RFC-style document for bringing an idea,
> change, or design question to the architecture group for discussion. It is the
> *input* to a decision — when consensus is reached, the outcome is captured in an ADR.
>
> Keep proposals short and focused. The goal is to generate productive discussion,
> not to be comprehensive. Unanswered questions belong in *Open Questions*, not
> buried in prose.
>
> **Do not use** when a decision has already been made — use `template_adr.md` instead.
> Do not use for large system descriptions — use `template_sad.md`.
>
> **Suggested location:** `decisions/proposed/`
> **File naming:** `proposal_[domain]_[short-title].md`
> **Status lifecycle:** Proposed → Accepted → Rejected → Retired
