> **Template Note** — Delete this block before publishing.
>
> **Document type:** `pattern` | **Diátaxis:** Procedure
>
> A Design Pattern documents a repeatable, approved approach to solving a specific
> architecture or infrastructure problem. It describes *how* to implement something,
> under what conditions, and what the expected outcome looks like.
>
> Keep patterns goal-oriented and steps actionable. Move rationale and background
> to a linked concept document — patterns are for *doing*, not *explaining*. If a
> step needs a "Note: the reason we do this is..." that explanation belongs in a
> linked overview or SAD.
>
> **Do not use** for operational procedures executed during incidents or maintenance
> windows — use `template_runbook.md` instead. Patterns are design-time artifacts.
>
> **Suggested location:** `patterns/[subdomain]/`
> **File naming:** `pattern_[domain]_[descriptor].md`
> **Status lifecycle:** Draft → In Review → Accepted → Retired
